AUTOMATION OF PETROL BUNK MANAGEMENT BY USING RFID TAGS:
Project's Objective:-

The main objective of this project is to provide easy access and reduce time in petrol bunks and to avoid the misconceptious arguments while billing paymenats.

Hardware required:-
  1. RFID reader
  2. DC moter
  3. Microcontroller
  4. digital meter
  5. buzzer
  6. keypad
Software required:-
  1. Embeded C
  2. Database
Proposed System:-

The designed pepared meter consist of of a microcontroller, RFID tags and digital meter. The proposed meter consists of RFID reader and server, the server located in the local sub-station the RFID reader can read the data of the credit card and sends back the udated information after studying the card to the client.

RFID tags:-
  • Active tags and
  • Passive tags
    • Active Tags:-
    Active tags don't contain the buit in battery,it can use the energy transmmitted by the RFID reader.
    Passive tags:-
    It contain the buitin battery and it uses its own energy.
DC Motor:-

It can converts the electrical energy into mechanical energy.

Applications:-
  1. It can be applied in industries to detect dreadful gases.
  2. It is applied to milk processing industries to get the quantity milk.
  3. It is also applied in water plants.

  • Provides easy acces to the customers.
